I presented a talk about “Jumping into digital: Lessons learned while moving live-coding workshops online” at the ARDC community webinar series in April 2020. The abstract of my talk was: At ResBaz 2019, I said (on camera) that it is impossible to run a hands-on, live-coding digital skills training workshop online. In March 2020, I led a team of trainers to do exactly this - move our 2-day, Carpentries-style, hands-on Machine Learning in R and Machine Learning in python workshops fully online - in a week!
